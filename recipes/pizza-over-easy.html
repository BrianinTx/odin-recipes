Pizza Over Easy

This recipe answers the age old question, "What do I do with left-over pizza?". Apparently, "Eat it dummy" is way too simple for our complex times. I invented this culinary monstosity on my honeymoon. Twenty plus years later, we are still married. Must be love.

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of leftover pizza
  • 2 Large eggs
  • Garlic salt
  • Left-over red paper flakes. You didn't throw those packets away did you?

Preparation

  1. If the pizza is thin crust, cut the back of the crust off so that the pizza will lie flat.
  2. Heat a non-stick or lightly oiled cast iron skillet over medium heat.
  3. Crack one egg into the skillet and lightly season with garlic salt and red pepper
  4. Place pizza, face-down on top of the egg. Cook for about one minute. The egg will take longer to cook because of the pizza.
  5. Remove pizza from skillet and set aside.
  6. Crack next egg into skillet and season as before.
  7. Place pizza face-up on top of egg. Cook for about one minute.
  8. Serve hot with you favorite breakfast beverage.
