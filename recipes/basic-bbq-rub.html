Basic BBQ Rub

If you are an old grill hand, you have probably used something like this before. This is my personal version. This is a great rub for beef, pork, and chicken. Rub it generously into brisket and ribs. Mix with ground beef for hamburgers. Rub it on and under the skin for poultry. I like to add a teaspoon to a bowl of canned chili to liven it up. If you have time, season your meat and place it in the refrigerator overnight to let the flavors really blend in.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup paprika
  • 4 tablespoons coarse/kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons celery seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix ingredients with you hands to break up the brown sugar.
  3. Refrigerate in an airtight jar for up to 6 months.
